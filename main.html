Greetings!

Today we will learn how to use the Star Wars API!

Why SWAPI?

With everyone excited about the new Star Wars movies, people want to know more about the universe their favorite characters are living in. The SWAPI has a lot of information ready to be plucked and used by us! Their documentation walks through a simple call and the base URL, but they don't show how we can get more information.

What are we going to learn?

Besides how to extract basic information from the SWAPI, we are going to make a list of all the planets in the database. We will then build on this list what movies those planets are featured in. Finally we will restructure our code to list the characters, their homeworlds, and what movies they appear in.
